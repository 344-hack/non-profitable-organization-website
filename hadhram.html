About Kenyan Hadharim!

Kenya Hadharim Self-Help Group, is a group that advocates for and supports Kenya Hadharim Community Members participation in development processes. We envision: A Community where our Members including our youth are empowered to realize their full potential. The KH-SHG currently has over hundred (100) Kenya Hadharim Members in different Counties in Kenya (Kitui, Kilifi, Nairobi, Wajir) and in seven (7) different Diaspora Countries (USA, UK, Qatar, Kuwait, UAE, Saudia & Uganda).

Our Subscription Plans

Method of Payments:

For your Registration for a Subscriber to be approved, a Member has to deposit his Admission and Annual Fees (Kshs. 500.00 + 365.00) with any choice of our two Plans (B or C). All payments should be deposited to the below accounts before the 10th day of each calendar month.
All payments for Monthly Membership (Plan B) make direct payment to GAB Pay bill no.: 985050 - Kshs. Account No. 0800070602.
For the Investment Membership (Plan C) make direct payment to KH-SHG Pay bill no.: 4092161 add your full names in place of Account No. - Account No. 0800070601.
For our Diaspora Members make transfer through GAB Swift Code: GAFRKEN

Once you have paid, add the MPESA CODE on form and submit.
